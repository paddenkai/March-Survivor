How It Works
🗺️

4 Quadrants

East, West, South, Midwest. Pick one team per quadrant each round — 4 total picks per round.

🚫

No Repeats

Once you use a team, it's off your board for the rest of the tournament. Choose wisely.

💀

Quadrant Elimination

Wrong pick = eliminated from that quadrant. You still earn points in your other quadrants.

🔢

Points Win

Every correct pick = +1 point. Most points at the end of the tournament wins the pot.

🏆

Last Alive Wins

Most total points wins the pot. Tiebreaker: most quadrants still alive. Still tied? Split it.

💸

$10 Entry

Pay via Venmo after registering. Unpaid entries are removed before picks lock.
